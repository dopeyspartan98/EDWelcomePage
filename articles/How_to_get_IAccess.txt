How to get IAccess (Tap Access)<br>
1. Fill out the IRRMSS_EHRApplication found at (site)(link). Make sure to ask questions if you need it and MAKE SURE TO CHECK THE IACCESS BOX.<br>
2. After the form is signed like it’s supposed to be, submit the form in a ticket at (site). Make sure to add all relevant information.<br> 
3. Give it a few business days to get processed, then go to Systems and tell them that you put in a request for IAccess.<br>
