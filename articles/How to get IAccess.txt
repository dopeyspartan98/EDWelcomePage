How to get IAccess (Tap Access)
1. Fill out the IRRMSS_EHRApplication found at (site)(link). Make sure to ask questions if you need it and MAKE SURE TO CHECK THE IACCESS BOX. 
2. After the form is signed like it’s supposed to be, submit the form in a ticket at (site). Make sure to add all relevant information. 
3. Give it a few business days to get processed, then go to Systems and tell them that you put in a request for IAccess.
