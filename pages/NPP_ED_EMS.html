EMS

Lakenheath EMS serves a tri-base area between RAF Lakenheath, RAF Mildenhall, and RAF Feltwell. The ALS unit is based on RAF Lakenheath and will respond to all three bases while there is a BLS unit on RAF Mildenhall embedded with the Fire Department. Travel between bases in the ambulance will happen WITHOUT lights and sirens, even in emergency situations, unless a LNDH member is driving. The UK requires a specific qualification to drive through UK local streets with lights and sirens, which the Air Force does not provide a course for.
